Wall-mounted fans
The wall-mounting man coolers are suitable for mounting at higher elevations in the wall to avoid the occupation of floor space. These fans are mounted on steel pipe with suitable holes in the base plates for heavy-duty motors.
Pedestal Mancoolers
These types of mancoolers are designed to stand on the shop floors. The cast iron solid base holds the assembly on the floor free from vibration. The air throw can be adjusted vertically for various positions.
Tubular Mancoolers
In these type of mancoolers, the impeller and motor assembly are mounted in the circular casing with a suitable stand arrangement. This can be moved from place to place easily even in rough floor areas.

Axial Flow Fan
Almonard's axial flow fans provide efficient, high-volume air movement for a wide range of industrial and commercial applications. These rugged, high-performance units are engineered to move large quantities of air over long distances.
Axial flow fans work by drawing air in parallel to the axis of the fan's impeller. The air is then accelerated and discharged in the same direction by the rotating blades. This straight-through airflow pattern makes them ideal for exhaust and ventilation systems.
